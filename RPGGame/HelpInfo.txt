
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Game objective: Kill everyone !!!
Attack enemy by enter in its position.
You can level up.
You can choose to increase damage (+30) or maximum health (+30) on level up.
Every enemy have different experiance reward:
- Fairy - 100 experience
- Ork - 150 experience
- Ninja - 200 experience

You can collect and equip a sword to increase the player damage.
Only one sword can be equipped at a time.
Every sword have different damage and durability.
One attack cost one sword durability.
The sword is broken (you can collect another one) when the durability reaches 0.

Characters info:
- P - current player position
- S - sword
- H - health potion (large, medium or small)
- O - ork (enemy)
- F - fairy (enemy)
- N - ninja (enemy)

Commands list:
- help - displays basic game info and commands.
- map - displays the map.
- move - enter player movement mode (use arrow keys on the keyboard to move - one click = one position move). Press ESC or any other key to quit this mode and enter new command.
- heal - heal the player. Choose health potion type to use (large = 100 points, medium = 75 points, small = 50 points).
- potions - display the current player potions info (large, medium, small).
- enemies - display info about the enemies on the map.
- clear - clears the screen.
- exit - run like a little girl !
